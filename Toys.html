Cat Toys


Do you know which toys cats will like the most?
One toy that a cat will like the most is a catnip toy. You can buy it online or make it at home. A cat will love batting it and knocking it down.
Another toy that cats like is a spring. The spring gets the cat excited, then it can "hunt" it and proudly carry it back!
If you are looking for a cheap simple toy you can get a ball of yarn. This classic toy is something that you cat will like a lot!
