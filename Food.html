What do cats eat?

Have you ever wondered what food cats eat?
Or have you ever given a cat milk but it didn't drink it?
Turns out, that kittens are lactose intolerant. Weird right?

If you're wondering what cats like the best, it's meat.
You can change your cat's diet by giving different kinds of meat or give it cooked or raw.
A cats favorite meat is usually fish but they'll eat other meats too.
You can also give cats veggie burgers as long as it doesn't onions or garlic.
